The Magic of New Tab

What Tab Out does, why it exists, and what happens the moment you open a new tab.

You built something pretty clever

Tab Out is a Chrome extension that replaces your boring new tab page with a living dashboard of everything you have open right now — grouped neatly by website, with one-click controls to close entire domains, flag duplicates, and save tabs for later.

Most people lose track of tabs the way they lose track of Post-it notes. Tab Out makes the chaos visible and manageable.

100% local — no server, no account

Most apps you build need a server running somewhere in the cloud. Tab Out has zero backend. It talks directly to Chrome's built-in APIs to do everything.

💡
Key Insight: Not every app needs a server

Browser extensions live inside Chrome and get special privileges — they can read tabs, store data, and modify pages without ever touching the internet. This makes them fast, private, and free to run. When you ask an AI to build your next tool, ask yourself: "Does this actually need a backend, or can it run entirely in the browser?"
